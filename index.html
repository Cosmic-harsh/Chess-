How to Play

  • Click on a piece to select it, then click on a highlighted square to move
  • When you check your opponent, they get a dare!
  • Capturing a piece triggers a dare for your opponent
  • Checkmate results in a special dare celebration!
  • Use the "Random Dare" button to send a dare anytime
